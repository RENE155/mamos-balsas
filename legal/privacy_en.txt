PRIVACY POLICY
MamosBalsas 
Last updated: 2025-01-20

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how the "MamosBalsas" (Bedtime Stories) app (hereinafter "App", "we", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. By using our App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

2.1. Account Data
- Unique user identifier
- Subscription status

2.2. Voice Data
- Voice recordings you provide for voice cloning
- Created voice profiles

2.3. Children's Profiles
- Child's name
- Child's age
- Interests (optional)

2.4. Generated Stories
- Story texts
- Audio recordings
- Illustrations

2.5. Technical Data
- Device type
- Operating system version
- App usage statistics

3. VOICE DATA PROCESSING

3.1. Third-Party Services
Your voice recordings are sent to ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io) - an artificial intelligence voice cloning service. ElevenLabs processes your voice and creates a voice profile used for reading stories.

3.2. ElevenLabs Data Handling
- ElevenLabs stores your voice data according to their privacy policy
- Voice data is used only for story generation
- When you delete a voice profile in our App, we also delete it from ElevenLabs systems

3.3. Consent
Before recording your voice, we request your explicit consent. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting the voice profile.

4. STORY GENERATION

4.1. OpenAI Services
Story texts and illustrations are generated using OpenAI artificial intelligence services. Data sent includes:
- Child's age
- Selected theme
- Interests (if provided)

4.2. Content Safety
All generated stories are checked by content safety filters to ensure child-appropriate content.

5. DATA STORAGE

5.1. Storage Location
Your data is stored on Supabase servers (supabase.com), which comply with GDPR requirements.

5.2. Retention Period
- Account data: while account is active or until you request deletion
- Voice profiles: until you delete them or delete your account
- Stories: until you delete them or delete your account

5.3. Data Deletion
You can at any time:
- Delete individual voice profiles
- Delete individual stories
- Delete your entire account and all associated data

6. YOUR RIGHTS

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

6.1. Right of Access
Obtain information about what data we process about you.

6.2. Right to Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate data.

6.3. Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").

6.4. Right to Restriction
Request restriction of your data processing.

6.5. Right to Data Portability
Receive your data in a structured format.

6.6. Right to Object
Object to processing of your data.

6.7. Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw given consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: crygml@gmail.com

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

7.1. Age Restrictions
The App is intended for parents and guardians who create stories for their children. Children should not use the App without adult supervision.

7.2. Children's Data
We collect only minimal children's data (name, age) necessary for creating personalized stories. This data is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
